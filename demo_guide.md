# FinWise AI Platform - Demo Recording Guide (2-Minute Script)

This document outlines the purpose of each component on the page and provides a step-by-step walkthrough to record a professional 2-minute demo video of the **FinWise AI** platform.

---

## 🏗️ Platform Component Guide

### 1. Header Ticker & Navigation Bar
* **Interactive Ticker**: Displays live financial status signals (e.g. NIFTY 50, SENSEX, Google Sheets sync connection state, and AI Engine status).
* **Navigation Links**: Multi-tab Single Page Application (SPA) routing that lets users seamlessly navigate without page reloads.
* **3D Particle Background**: Realistic, tumbling 3D Bitcoin canvas engine that shifts colors (Sky Blue, Purple, Emerald, Gold) and maintains premium depth visuals.

### 2. Loan Checker Tab (Applicant Profile)
* **Form Inputs**: Collects applicant name, monthly salary, credit score, existing EMIs, and requested loan amount.
* **Results Panel**: An eligibility summary display showing approval status, credit risk rating, and maximum eligible limit.
* **Interactive Audio Feedback**: Synthesizes an ascending chord arpeggio for approved loans, or a warning chime for rejections.

### 3. Credit Analyzer Tab (Score Impact Analyzer)
* **Interactive Gauge**: Visualizes FICO categories (Poor, Fair, Good, Excellent) using dynamic color-coded ranges.
* **Key Impact Progress Bars**: Breaks down metrics like payment history (35%) and utilization (30%) based on the input score.
* **Actionable Recommendations**: Displays customized score-building advice.
* **Jump to AI Link**: Redirects parameters directly to the AI Chatbot for detailed instructions.

### 4. EMI Calculator Tab
* **Interactive Sliders**: Principal amount, interest rates, and loan tenure sliders with real-time numeric text updates.
* **Audio Micro-Ticks**: Modern click sounds play instantly on slider adjustments.
* **Repayment Schedule**: A tabular log detailing month-by-month principal weight, interest components, and closing balances.

### 5. AI Advisory Console
* **⚡ Quick Presets**: Side menu offering pre-composed finance queries (e.g. budget rules, consolidation).
* **Chat Window**: Beautiful messaging UI with typing indicators ("Claude is reasoning...") and markdown headers formatting.
* **Control Actions**: Top actions allowing you to clear conversation history or return back to the Credit Analyzer tab.

## 📹 2-Minute Demo Recording Script

Follow this workflow to record a clean, concise, and engaging 2-minute product video:

### Phase 1: Intro & Nav Bar Visuals (0:00 - 0:25)
* **Action**: Start on the home screen. Show the falling 3D coins rotating in the background. Hover your cursor over the navigation links (listen to the subtle navigation sound) and point out the live status ticker in the top right showing `"FINWISE ENGINE: Active"`.
* **Talking Points**: 
  > *"Welcome to FinWise AI, an intelligent credit decisioning and financial advisory platform. The background displays our custom 3D floating coins engine, and the top bar tracks the real-time API connection and sheets synchronization states."*

### Phase 2: Loan Eligibility Test (0:25 - 0:55)
* **Action**: Navigate to **Loan Checker**. Leave the default values (Salary: 45K, Score: 730, EMIs: 0, Loan: 9L) and click **Evaluate Loan Eligibility**. Show the success toast and the Approved status with Low Risk.
* **Talking Points**: 
  > *"Here, we evaluate loan applicant profiles in real-time. By submitting this form, the rule-engine calculates a Debt-To-Income ratio of 37% and marks the applicant as Approved with Low Risk, playing a celebratory approval chime."*

### Phase 3: Credit Analyzer (0:55 - 1:25)
* **Action**: Click **Explore Credit Score Analyzer** button inside the Loan Checker tab to jump tabs. The FICO score input is automatically pre-filled. Click **Analyze**. Show the progress metrics change. Click **Ask AI Tips for Score Improvement**.
* **Talking Points**: 
  > *"Our Credit Analyzer categorizes the applicant's creditworthiness. Clicking 'Ask AI Tips' immediately passes FICO parameters to the AI Advisory Console to generate a customized credit recovery blueprint."*

### Phase 4: AI Advisor Console & Chatbot (1:25 - 1:45)
* **Action**: The chat tab opens, shows the reasoning spinner, and renders the Markdown roadmap. Highlight the new chatbot header. Click **Clear Chat** (watch the history reset) and then click **Back to Analyzer**.
* **Talking Points**: 
  > *"The Advisory Console, powered by Claude AI, provides structured wealth blueprints. We can easily clear our chat conversation or return back to our previous page in one click using the header controls."*

### Phase 5: EMI Calculator & Summary (1:45 - 2:00)
* **Action**: Navigate to **EMI Calculator**. Drag the Principal slider (hear the micro-click sounds) and scroll through the dynamic amortization table rows.
* **Talking Points**: 
  > *"Lastly, our Loan EMI Calculator helps simulate principal variables with micro-acoustic feedback, rendering full payment amortization schedules dynamically. Thank you for watching!"*
